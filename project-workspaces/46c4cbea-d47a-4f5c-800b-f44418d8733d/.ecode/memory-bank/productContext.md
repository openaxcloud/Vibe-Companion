# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Small businesses need an affordable, feature-rich platform to sell online without technical complexity or high transaction fees.

## Target Users
**Primary**: Small business owners (vendors) seeking online sales channels
**Secondary**: End consumers looking for unique products from local businesses
**Tertiary**: Platform administrators managing marketplace operations

## UX Goals
- Intuitive product discovery with smart search and filtering
- Frictionless checkout process (guest and registered users)
- Clear order tracking and communication
- Seamless vendor onboarding and product management

## Key User Flows
1. **Buyer Journey**: Browse → Search/Filter → Add to Cart → Checkout → Order Tracking
2. **Vendor Journey**: Register → Product Upload → Inventory Management → Order Fulfillment
3. **Admin Journey**: User Management → Platform Analytics → Dispute Resolution
