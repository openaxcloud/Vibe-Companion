# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Small-to-medium businesses need an accessible platform to sell products online without building custom e-commerce infrastructure, while customers need a unified shopping experience across multiple vendors.

## Target Users
**Primary**: Small business owners/vendors seeking online sales channels
**Secondary**: End customers looking for diverse product selection
**Tertiary**: Marketplace administrators managing platform operations

## UX Goals
- Intuitive product discovery through smart search and filtering
- Frictionless checkout process with guest and registered user flows
- Clear order tracking and communication
- Seamless vendor onboarding and product management

## Key User Flows
1. **Customer Journey**: Browse → Search/Filter → Add to Cart → Checkout → Order Tracking
2. **Vendor Journey**: Register → Product Upload → Inventory Management → Order Fulfillment
3. **Admin Journey**: User Management → Platform Analytics → Dispute Resolution

## Success Indicators
- <2 minute average checkout time
- >80% cart completion rate
- <5% order dispute rate
